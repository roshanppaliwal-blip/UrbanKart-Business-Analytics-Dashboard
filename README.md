# 🛒 UrbanKart Business Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Data Analytics case study** for a 5-year-old e-commerce startup, *UrbanKart*. The goal was to analyze large-scale transactional data (~400,000 records) to uncover insights related to **revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and operational efficiency**.

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where a data analyst is required to clean, analyze, and visualize data to support decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze revenue and profit trends over time
* Identify high-performing and underperforming products
* Understand customer purchasing behavior and retention
* Evaluate return rates and operational inefficiencies
* Provide actionable insights to improve business performance

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Description

The project consists of 4 datasets:

* **Orders**: Transaction-level data (Sales, Profit, Discount, Region, etc.)
* **Customers**: Customer demographics and join date
* **Products**: Product categories and pricing
* **Returns**: Return records with reasons

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning (Excel)

* Removed duplicates and handled missing values
* Standardized formats (dates, text fields)
* Created derived columns (Year, Month)
* Validated profit and discount values

---

## 🧮 SQL Analysis (MySQL)

Performed advanced queries to extract insights:

* Monthly revenue trends
* Profit margin by category
* Top 10% customers contribution
* Region-wise performance
* Return rate analysis

---

## 📊 Power BI Dashboard

### 🔹 Page 1: Executive Overview

* KPI Cards (Revenue, Profit, Orders, Profit Margin)
* Monthly revenue trend
* Region-wise performance

### 🔹 Page 2: Product Analysis

* Revenue & Profit by category
* Profit margin comparison
* High revenue but low profit products
* Top-performing products

### 🔹 Page 3: Customer Insights

* Top customers by revenue
* Repeat vs one-time customers
* Customer segmentation
* Pareto Analysis (80/20 rule)

### 🔹 Page 4: Returns & Operations

* Return rate by category & region
* Return reasons analysis
* Operational inefficiencies identification

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* A small segment of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue (Pareto principle observed)
* Electronics category generates highest revenue but operates at relatively lower profit margins
* Certain high-selling products show low profitability due to discounting
* Return rate (~12%+) indicates operational inefficiencies
* Specific regions show higher return rates, suggesting logistics or service issues
